A Sweet Scent in the Air

Marissa Zappas, a perfumer based in Brooklyn, is currently serving as a year-long-artist-in-residence at the Elsewhere music venue in Bushwick. As part of the residency, each season of the year she is scenting a space in the venue.

Marissa Zappas is working on an exhibition, set to launch October 27th, that will feature the smells of jasmine and myrrh.
Zappas is originally from California, though she came to New York to study for school. .
The art gallery at Elsewhere is unique, as it is above the music venue. Mollly Surno serves as the director of the gallery.
The shows at the are gallery are actually subsidized through the club's music ticket sales.
